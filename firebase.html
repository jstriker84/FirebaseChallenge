Online Boggle Game

Press play and invite friends to compete with you in real-time. Make as many words with the random letters as you can in one minute. Order of the letters doesn't matter, but each letter can be used only once per word. Once a word has been entered, it cannot be entered again by any other player. The player with the most words wins 10 points. The player with the word that uses the most letters wins 8 points.
